Tatyana E. TIMKINA
The member of the Russian Artists Union

Was born in 1961 in Kirov, Russia.

A graduate of an art school and the Kirov Art College (1987).
Studies under guidance of Piotr. S. Vershigorov & Eugeny A. Grishin.

In 1995 graduater from St.Petersburg's Academic institute of Painting, Sculpture
& Architecture named after I.E.Repin, personal workshop of A.A. Pahomov.

Also studies in the workshop of I.V.Balakova - a successor of water-colour school
of  P.P.Chistyakov  and V.E.Savinsky. 

Her diploma work "Still Life with Old Weapons" is on display at the permanent exposition of the museum of the Academy of Art in St.Petersburg.

Now work as a teacher of drawing in the Russian innovations University. In her art gives preference
to water-colours; also works in book graphics.

Her water-colours are in private collections in Austria, Italy, UK, Sweden, USA, Czechia, Germany,
Japan, Poland.
